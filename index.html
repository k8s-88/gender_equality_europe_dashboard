A Measure of Gender Equality in Europe
Welcome to my dashboard which displays a variety of gender equality statistics across different graphs. All data was collected and analysed by the European Commission and the European Institute for Gender Equality.

This dashboard is split into two sections, stats and questions. The stats section contains a number of graphs representing data on general gender equality issues while the second displays data on the opinions of people in Europe on such issues.

Select a country(or countries) on any of the graphs if you wish to compare them and these countries will be on display across all graphs. The Reset All button will bring the graphs back to default posistion.
